September 11 Memorial Fund

In Memory

We honor the victims, the heroes who responded, and the families forever changed by this tragedy. Today, we continue their legacy through action, supporting first responders, veterans, and families who need our help.

How Your Donation Helps

  • Emergency Financial Assistance - Immediate financial relief to families of fallen first responders and veterans, covering essential expenses like housing, utilities, and daily necessities during their greatest need
  • Healthcare Support - Comprehensive medical care and treatment for survivors and first responders suffering from 9/11-related illnesses, including respiratory conditions and cancer treatment
  • Educational Scholarships - College scholarships for children and dependents of victims and first responders, ensuring their educational dreams remain achievable despite family tragedy
  • Mental Health Services - Professional counseling and psychological support services for survivors, families, and first responders dealing with trauma and grief from the attacks

Donations are processed and distributed directly to verified 9/11 memorial organizations. 100% of proceeds go to beneficiaries. No fees.
